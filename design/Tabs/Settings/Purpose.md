Purpose: Personalized configuration of the PromptStudio environment and user account.





Account & Profile: Centralized management for user identity (Display Name, Email) and avatar.



Theme Preferences: Visual selectors for interface modes ("Deep Obsidian," "Steel Slate," "Ghost White") to suit different lighting conditions.



Workspace Defaults: Global overrides for the AI behavior, such as the "Default AI Model," "Auto-Archive" frequency, and "Contextual Memory" toggles.



Security & Sessions: Right Sidebar tracking of active devices and security audits to ensure the workspace remains private.
